# Founder essay playbook — weekly “why CurXor”

> **Room:** **Founder Public Voice** · `.cursor/skills/founder-public-voice/`

> **Channels:** X public · LinkedIn stealth · other social idle — see [STEALTH-MODE.md](STEALTH-MODE.md)  
> **Daily posts:** same chat — say `daily X post` · copy-paste blocks delivered in chat

---

## What this is

The X article ([v1](x-article-why-im-building-curxor.md)) is not a tombstone. It is **week zero**.

Each week you capture:

1. **One real conversation** (coffee, DM, Boardy, family, operator)
2. **One signal from X** (reply, quote-tweet, competitor launch, macro thread)
3. **One honest build update** (what shipped, what blocked, what surprised you)

Then rewrite the **opening hook** and **one middle section**. Keep origin story + architecture + honest status as stable spine unless something materially changed.

**Primary ship target:** @ankurmisra (founder essay + thread). @curxorai reposts or product-framed variant.
