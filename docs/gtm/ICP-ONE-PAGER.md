## Deck slide (copy-paste)

### Title
**Ideal Customer — Act I**

### Headline
**The under-leveraged operator**

### Body (3 bullets)
- **Who:** Solo founder, consultant, or creator-operator — 28–45 — already uses AI daily but stuck at shallow tasks (the *grammar gap*)
- **Job to be done:** Run capital rules, content pipeline, and outbound sequences **24/7 on metal they own** — without API rent or weekend assembly
- **Why now:** Chat tab proved demand; nobody ships the **appliance** — CurXor is $3,999 once · $0/mo API for the operate plane

### Wedge (one line)
**Outreach · Creator · Capital** — Tier A exit-demo depth is the buy decision; Cafe and Signal are moat, not launch lead.

### Anti-ICP (footer, small type)
Not enterprise IT · not hobbyists · not "ChatGPT in a box" · not buyers who need all ten Claws production-grade on day one.

## Speaker notes (60 seconds)

1. **Open with the grammar gap** — your best prospects already use AI every day and feel under-leveraged. They're not laggards; they're stuck at the chat ceiling.

2. **Name Alex** — solo operator, stitches five tools, nothing stays on when life happens. $3,999 is a filter for someone who will actually run the desk.

3. **Wedge is three Claws** — Work, Creator, Capital. That's what they touch in week one. We're honest about five Coming Soon — trust beats theater at this price.

4. **Economics** — own once vs recurring API rent. Operate plane is $0/mo API; frontier LLM is opt-in. Sovereignty is rational, not ideological, for this buyer.

5. **Expansion** — Kin/Vital for household lock-in; Signal for device thesis; L4/L5 agency/executive after L3 operators prove the appliance. Sequence matters.

6. **Close** — "We're not selling the orchestrator thesis to cold traffic. We're selling a box with ten agents. The thesis is what makes them stay."

## Qualification checklist (sales / waitlist)

**Strong fit**
- [ ] Uses AI weekly+ and names a workflow they want always-on
- [ ] Already pays for 2+ tools in outbound, creator, or trading
- [ ] Comfortable with $3,999 as hardware + software purchase
- [ ] Solo or household principal — not buying for 50-seat IT

**Weak fit**
- [ ] Wants only chat, no vertical desks
- [ ] Needs enterprise SSO / compliance day one
- [ ] Expects all ten Claws production-complete
- [ ] Primary interest is humanoid / VR with no Work/Creator/Capital use case
