## Sequencing rules

1. **P1 before P2/P3 scale** — prove Alex delights before wedge campaigns multiply.
2. **Tier A depth before audience expansion** — Cafe moat doesn't replace Work/Creator/Capital proof.
3. **S1/S2 on nurture tracks** — not homepage parity with flagship claws.
4. **F1/F2 after L3 density** — agency and executive are expansion, not launch.
5. **Hardware validation** before broad "sovereign appliance" paid claims.
